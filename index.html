The Lovett Film Festival

Going on vacation? Studying for exams? Don't let all of those beautiful, blue links go to waste. Each day, RedditLogger records the seven top links of ten subreddits of your choosing, allowing you access to a Reddit timeline upon your return. Written in PHP, this site essentially connects to the Reddit API, logs links from the subreddits listed in a .txt file, and stores them with MySQL. The logging process occurs each day at 10PM, and is executed by setcronjob.com. The account making process was something I had never before attempted. It essentially just verifies all the inputs one or five times, checks that all the subreddits actually exist, and adds them to the .txt file for the logger if they aren't already there. Cookies are used for the login process, and store only a serialized array of subreddits. The website currently resides on this server, though it will soon be moved to its own domain and server. Enjoy!
